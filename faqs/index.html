Publish Errors

You attempted to publish a project and received a publish error, here are some common causes and remedies:

• The project no longer exists online and may have been removed.

This may have been caused due to the owner of the project removing the project from their online account or a "publishedId" may have been manually entered into the settings that does not exist. You can fix this issue by updating the settings file and changing "publishedId" to null eg. "publishedId": null,. Publishing again will automatically populate the settings with a new "publishedId".

• You do not have the necessary rights to publish this project.

This can happen if you attempt to publish a project and you do not have the necessary privillages to publish online. You will need to speak with the owner of the project and request access or update your account.

• The service is down / your connection is down.

If the Mixture service is having issues this may affect you publishing your project online. We announce issues via our Twitter account. Please also check you have a connection to the internet.
